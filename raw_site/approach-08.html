Enrichment Programs

Enrichment opportunities abound for children who attend Kinderplatz. Special programs are offered to all age groups throughout the Center by on-staff specialists, at little to no additional cost.

Programs provided as part of weekly tuition are: Spanish Immersion Program (preschool/pre-k choice opt-in program), Spanish Language Instruction, Music Classes, Creative Movement, Sign Language, some In-House Events, as well as our Cultural Enrichment Program that takes us on pretend journeys all over the world, each ending in a center-wide family celebration event.

Programs provided for an affordable, additional fee are: Kinderroo Soccer (during the summer months), Field Trips, and some In-House Events.
