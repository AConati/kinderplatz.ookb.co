Kinderplatz is open, licensed and insured from 6:30am-6:00pm.* All rates include breakfast, lunch and snacks (infant formula is not included), and some special programs. As a facility supported by the GSA (General Services Administration), young children of Federal families receive reduced tuition rates. Activity fees are applicable to Kinderoo Soccer and some field trips/in-house events, and are included in special packages or charged separately. We also accept families who receive child care subsidies from various county or government assistance programs, including Military Subsidy programs. Please contact one of the directors for more information.
